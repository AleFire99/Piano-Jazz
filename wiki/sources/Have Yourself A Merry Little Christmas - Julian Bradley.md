# Have Yourself A Merry Little Christmas — Julian Bradley

**Format:** PDF (notated piano arrangement)
**Author/Teacher:** Julian Bradley
**Core focus:** Complete jazz piano arrangement of the Christmas standard, demonstrating how all previously taught concepts apply to an actual lead sheet performance

## Key takeaways

- The arrangement is a practical synthesis document: it shows shell voicings, rootless voicings, tritone substitutions, approach notes, and block chords all deployed in a real musical context.
- Demonstrates **reading a lead sheet in real time**: taking a chord symbol + melody and generating a stylistically appropriate piano part.
- Shows how **tritone substitutions** appear naturally in a standard (e.g., replacing V7 with the sub-dominant a tritone away at phrase endings).
- Illustrates **voice leading between voicings**: how rootless voicings connect smoothly from chord to chord by moving as few notes as possible.
- Good beginner-to-intermediate model: not overly dense, but already uses 4-note voicings throughout.

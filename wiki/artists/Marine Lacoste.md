# Marine Lacoste

**Role:** Piano educator / author
**Focus:** Blues piano fundamentals — left-hand patterns, form, and rhythmic feels

## Sources ingested

- [[10 Left Hand Blues Patterns - Marine Lacoste]] — 12-bar blues form, 10 LH patterns, walking bass

## Concepts taught

- [[12-Bar Blues]] — form, dominant 7th chord structure, turnaround
- Walking bass — quarter-note bass lines connecting chord tones
- Blues left-hand patterns — 10 patterns from whole-note roots to full boogie-woogie
- Chromatic approach (♭III → III) — defining blues bass gesture

## Style notes

Marine Lacoste's approach is methodical and accessible: each pattern is introduced on its own before being combined. Her material is a solid entry point for the blues domain before moving into jazz improvisation over blues forms.

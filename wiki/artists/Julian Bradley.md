# Julian Bradley

Jazz pianist and educator. Founder of **Jazz Tutorial** (jazztutorial.com). Known for clear, systematic teaching of jazz piano concepts with practical application focus.

---

## Teaching style

- Demonstrates everything from C, then shows how to adapt to all chord types and all 12 keys
- Emphasizes **interval patterns** over note memorization
- Advocates learning one concept at a time to depth before moving on
- Strong focus on **practical application**: every concept is tied to when and how to use it

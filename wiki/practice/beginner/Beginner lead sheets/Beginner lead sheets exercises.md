
## **Tips for Practicing Lead Sheets**

- Start with simpler songs and work your way up to more complex jazz standards.  
- Focus on playing the chords in a rhythmically consistent way, using different patterns for variation.  
- Practice improvising over the chord changes to develop your ability to fill in harmonic gaps.  
- Record yourself playing from lead sheets to track your progress and identify areas for improvement.

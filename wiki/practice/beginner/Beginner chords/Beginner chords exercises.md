
## **Tips for Practicing Chords**

- Focus on smooth, clean transitions between chords.  
- Start slow and work up to speed while maintaining clarity.  
- Practice both hands separately before playing together.  
- Experiment with different progressions to develop flexibility.  
- Use a metronome to practice chord changes in time with the beat.


## **Tips for Practicing Voicings**
- Start with basic voicings and gradually add complexity.  
- Pay attention to the distance between the notes of the voicing to ensure clarity.  
- Practice both closed and open voicings to understand different textural effects.  
- Focus on smooth transitions between chords by prioritizing voice leading.  
- Experiment with different inversions and drops to find your preferred sound.
